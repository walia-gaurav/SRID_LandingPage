Communicating with Team Members

Talk to your team members at any time during the mission. Use images and videos to facilitate communication. Enabled with a stable and high speed Emergency Wifi set up by drone routers.

Locating Team Members for help!

GIVEN a rescuer needs extra help in a particular part of the mission, WHEN the rescuer accesses the device and navigates for near-by-help feature, THEN the device presents an option to broadcast this request with a precise location of the callee.

Monitoring health status of rescuer

Continuously monitoring health of the rescuers is important to conduct a safe mission. With our solution, whenever any certain health parameter drops, the system will alarm the health team member immediately. Medical help decision can be made within seconds and a smooth operation is ensured at the same time.

Finding Best Digging Routes

Finding entry points to collapsed structures can be very complicated and our application provides one-click solution. These suggestions are based on data from onsite sensors and up-to-date blueprints.
